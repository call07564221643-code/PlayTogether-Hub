What users get

Resources for sustainable inclusive activities

The platform is designed for any school, playground or professional setting that wants repeatable, safe and inclusive play activities.

Activity Cards

Step-by-step cards with time, headcount, sensory risks, non-verbal options and safeguarding notes.

Training Material

Short professional modules for staff, parents, caregivers and playground facilitators.

Safeguarding Layer

Adult prompts covering distress, anxiety, sensory overload, peer pressure and safe stopping points.

Sustainable Theme

Reusable activities for classrooms, playgrounds, clubs, family support and community inclusion.
